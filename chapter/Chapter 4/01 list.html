Staps to bake cake

1. Preparation

  1. Read the recipe: Understand all steps and ingredients first.
  2. Preheat oven: Usually to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Prepare pan: Grease and flour your cake pan, or line with parchment paper.
  4. Gather ingredients: Use room temperature butter, eggs, and milk for better mixing.

2. Mixing the Batter

  • Cream butter & sugar: Beat softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  • Add eggs & vanilla: Beat in eggs one at a time, then mix in vanilla extract.
  • Combine dry ingredients: Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl.
  • Combine wet & dry: Alternate adding the dry mixture and milk to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined and smooth.

3. Baking & Cooling

Pour batter:
Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake:
Bake for the time specified in your recipe, usually 30-45 minutes.
Check for doneness:
A toothpick inserted in the center should come out clean.
Cool:
Let cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes on a wire rack, then invert onto the rack to cool completely before frosting.
